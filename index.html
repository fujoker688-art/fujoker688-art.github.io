About Us

SunBirch Media Limited is a Hong Kong-based professional multimedia service provider. We combine creative design, video production, and digital communication to deliver high-quality visual solutions for our clients.

Our brand name reflects our core philosophy: "Sun" represents fire and light - the spark of creativity and illumination. "Birch" represents wood and stability - a solid foundation for growth. Together, wood fuels fire, symbolizing sustainable creative energy and lasting business relationships.

We uphold professionalism, innovation, and excellence, providing one-stop visual communication solutions across multiple industries.

Our Promise

To transform ideas into compelling visual stories that resonate with audiences and elevate brands in the digital age.
