About Us

曦樺多媒體有限公司（SunBirch Media Limited），是立足香港的專業多媒體服務機構，結合創意設計、影視製作、數位傳播於一體，致力為客戶打造高品質視覺傳播方案。

SunBirch Media Limited is a Hong Kong-based professional multimedia service provider. We combine creative design, video production, and digital communication to deliver high-quality visual solutions for our clients.

品牌命名蘊含「曦（火）·光影傳播，樺（木）·根基穩固」之意，木火相生，事業根基穩固、創意生生不息。

Our brand name reflects our core philosophy: "Sun" represents fire and light - the spark of creativity and illumination. "Birch" represents wood and stability - a solid foundation for growth.

我們秉持專業、創新、精緻的服務理念，深耕多媒體行業，為客戶提供一站式視覺傳播解決方案。

We uphold professionalism, innovation, and excellence, providing one-stop visual communication solutions across multiple industries.
